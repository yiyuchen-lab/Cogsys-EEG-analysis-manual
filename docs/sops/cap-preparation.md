# Cap Preparation

???+ warning "Draft SOP"
    This page should be adapted to the exact cap model, electrode layout, and lab preparation routine.

## Checklist

- Confirm participant comfort and explain the procedure.
- Select the correct cap size.
- Align Cz according to the chosen measurement convention.
- Check frontal, central, parietal, temporal, and occipital electrode positions.
- Apply gel carefully and avoid bridging between neighboring electrodes.
- Document any electrodes that are difficult to prepare.

## Quality control

- Keep impedance consistent across channels.
- Avoid excessive gel.
- Re-check cap position after participant movement.
